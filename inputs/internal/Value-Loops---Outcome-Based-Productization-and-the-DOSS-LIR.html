Value Loops: outcome-based productization and the DOSS Leading Indicator of Retention

DOSS sells a system of record but delivers value only when customer transactions flow through it automatically. Every confirmed churn either never reached automatic transactional throughput or lost it. The model: unbundle install sequencing (not pricing) into productized Value Loops, land in the transactional path in week 1 on a day-1 rail, define go-live as the loop's activation predicate at the external boundary, and run the LIR on transactional throughput.

1 · What the evidence says

Churn is a throughput failure, not a fit failure

Across 8 confirmed churns and every silent account: the customer never reached the state where real transactional volume (orders, POs, invoices, journal entries) ran through DOSS automatically, or reached it and lost it (SL Munson: Excel dependency at 7 months post-live; Echelon: bundle orders never flowed). The loudest behavioral precursor is work leaving DOSS: parallel spreadsheets and reverts to the old system (ABA "back to fully manual" then 139+ days silent; Popdarts shadow Sheets over a PO-editing gap).

"I only see 4 SOs in the system which were manually created. Shouldn't we be seeing all orders flowing into DOSS?"
MHH, Jan 13 2026

The value moment is a first successful end-to-end transaction

Sauz's first clean close (Feb COGS pushed DOSS → QBO) flipped the account Off Track → On Track the same week; Tersus hit self-sufficient invoicing Jun 1; Smalls' first co-man POs went live Mar 24; Verve's daily lot creation grew "truly exponential." Go-live is not the value event: Magic Mind went live Apr 1 and rolled back Jun 8. Narrow scope is fast (Final Boss Sour: ~6 weeks to live inventory); multi-module installs run 6-10 months to first value, and two churns were explicitly speed-driven (My Dad's Chips, Prime Matter Labs $250K).

False TTX and the runner-SUCCEEDED trap (install-map fleet telemetry)

The first transaction always lands at order ingest, a cheap read rail (Ark 56 SPS polls/wk; Pavoi 2.4M sales orders), while the value-bearing outbound/posting leg goes live last or never (Ark QBO invoice 0 runs/30d, 855 acks parked; De Soi 856 ASN 1 run/180d vs 38 Pending-ASN; Magic Mind 856 zero runtime; Ozlo QBO dormant 93 days). Reading someone else's orders is not being in the transactional path. And DOSS runner status is not the transaction: Mezcla freight tenders 100% SUCCEEDED while failing at the broker; Ark's April 855 complete in DOSS, never received by SPS. Truth is read at the external boundary: partner accepted the document, the GL posted the entry, the 3PL count matches.

2 · The model: Value Loops [PROPOSED]

A Value Loop is one transaction cycle productized end-to-end. Loops have names, never letters (letters collide with the SPICED archetypes).

The four doctrines

  • Two rails per loop. Every loop ships with a day-1 rail (native form, AI document parsing, CSV, email) and a durable rail (EDI, GL sync, 3PL API) producing the identical DOSS record. The durable rail swaps in underneath records that already exist. Consequence for the LIR: documents processed in DOSS count as throughput regardless of rail; the churn anti-signal is specifically work leaving DOSS.
  • Landing. One-click read-only ingest (Shopify / SPS / 3PL / QBO → BigQuery; ZebraDB landing zone later, registry/views as the stable contract) at kickoff or late-stage POC. Mapping starts day 1; the customer sees their own unified data in week 1; master-data work gets its inputs.
  • Master data is a gate, not a loop. Loops are flows; master data is stock. SKU/BOM/vendor coverage is a setup gate inside every loop's activation, seeded by Landing, maintained by the transactions themselves (SoT by accretion).
  • Packaging unchanged. Contracts stay suite-shaped; loops unbundle delivery sequencing, not pricing. The order form names the lead loop and its business objective so the program is contract-anchored to an outcome.

5 · The LIR [PROPOSED]

LIR v1: P% of live ICP customers auto-process ≥ 5 transactional documents (SOs + POs + invoices/journal syncs) through DOSS every week, with zero work-leaving-DOSS events in the trailing 30 days.
  • E counts documents processed in DOSS on any rail (a parsed PDF PO is throughput). The anti-signal is work leaving DOSS: shadow spreadsheets, reverts, export spikes, manual-creation ratio breaches.
  • T = week for transactional loops; the Close loop overlays a monthly E (close executed in DOSS without Excel).
  • P set from a 12-week baseline (Roberge band 60-80%), measured against the ratified ICP only. The engagement proxy already separates near-perfectly (every account active ≥6 of 8 weeks is off the watchlist; 33 of 35 watchlist/churned at zero).
  • Maturity ladder: setup E (first e2e transaction, 2 consecutive cycles = the go-live gate) → engagement E (weekly cadence = the LIR) → ROI E (outcome receipts: hours avoided, close days, chargebacks at zero, freight saved vs benchmark).

6 · L2L instrumentation [PROPOSED, spec exists]

Full spec: l2l-app/docs/l2l-value-loops-instrumentation.md (companion to the L2L playbook data model; handed to the L2L workstream owner). Summary: BusinessObjective (customer's outcome verbatim, typed, criticalDate + consequence + counterparty required) captured at DISC and enforced at the KO gate, the discovery Appendix G handoff made structural, closing the churn-predictor-#1 gap ("hard deadline known to sales, not documented for implementation"). LoopDeclaration binds objectives to a lead loop with an activation predicate concrete to the customer's integrations, evaluated at the external boundary via a per-customer Loop Metric Registry entry. Stage wiring: BLD targets the first sentinel transaction (TTX clock inside build) → GL gate = predicate holds one full cycle → HC exit = two consecutive cycles, zero fallback (TTA) → SUS = LIR enrollment with an agent-run cadence watcher (the ni/mo:out exit). Gate conversion is cheap: the install maps' "Evidence required to close" cells are ~80% of the predicate text; Pavoi and Eight Sleep gates are already data-shaped.
