# DOSS public positioning, product, and traction

**Source:** doss.com (home, /products/platform), Series B press coverage. Researched 2026-07-06.

## Positioning (website)

- Headline: **"DOSS Operations Cloud is the ERP evolved."** Framing: "ERP is broken. To fix it, we created something completely new."
- Claims: **"Deploy 3x faster"**, "Make changes on the fly," "Speed up every step of your value chain by 10x or more." Configuration in weeks vs. 12–18 month consultant-led implementations.

## Product architecture (four components)

1. **ARP (Adaptive Resource Platform)** — "the first truly composable ERP alternative," built on three no-code blocks: **tables, forms, and workflows**. "Mix and match these blocks to fit your real-world processes." Spreadsheet-like tables as system of record.
2. **IDP (Integrated Data Platform)** — real-time data layer, **master data unification**, "modeling and reconciling complex relationships" across systems.
3. **DataStudio** — built-in real-time BI.
4. **Dossbot** — AI copilot: "query, analyze and automate via chat"; report building, data correction, bulk operations. Public AI detail is thin beyond copilot framing.

## Market & traction

- Target: mid-market physical goods — consumer goods, food & bev, health & beauty, manufacturing, distribution; **$20–250M revenue**.
- **Series B: $55M** (total $73M). Co-leads Madrona + Premji Invest; strategic: **Intuit Ventures**, Greyhound, Commerce Ventures. ~38 hires in 8 months incl. VP Sales from NetSuite/Coupa.
- Press positioning (Series B): **middleware that "plugs into ERP systems"** and integrates with QuickBooks — land-beside, not rip-and-replace. (Note the dual positioning vs. the website's "ERP alternative.")
- Named customers + proof: **Verve Coffee Roasters** (20+ hrs/wk saved), **Mezcla** (12+ hrs/wk, 2x faster POs), **Spread The Love** (12x faster invoicing, 92% time saved on order processing). All proof points are *time-savings/efficiency* metrics, not value-chain outcomes.

## Signal

- DOSS's own pitch is literally built on "tables, forms, workflows" — the exact surface Kurt flags as insufficient differentiation → **H1** (sharpens it: composability vs. adaptivity)
- Company already targets the mid-market ops value chain; Intuit Ventures + QuickBooks-integration stance → **H2**, **H3**
- Website *does* lead with value (10x value chain, hours saved) — the gap Kurt heard is in the sales motion, not marketing copy → **H4**
- "Deploy 3x faster / weeks not months" claim exists; Dossbot is copilot-level today; dynamic schema evolution not visible publicly → **H5** (the open frontier)
- Proof economics are efficiency stories; no inventory-turns / working-capital / stockout outcomes cited yet → new question for STRATEGY.md
